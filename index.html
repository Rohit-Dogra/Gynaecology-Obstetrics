🩺 Our Treatments

Nurturing Life, Caring for Women

Advanced, evidence-based treatment for every women's health need.

🤰

Pregnancy & Antenatal Care

Comprehensive monitoring of normal and high-risk pregnancies from conception through delivery. Our systematic prenatal programme ensures the health of both mother and baby at every stage.

  • Month-by-month prenatal consultations & monitoring
  • High-risk pregnancy management & specialist referrals
  • Ultrasound scans, blood tests & anomaly screening
  • Nutrition, fitness & lactation counselling
  • Normal delivery & planned cesarean section
  • Postpartum recovery & newborn care support
🔬

Laparoscopic & Minimally Invasive Surgery

State-of-the-art minimally invasive gynaecological surgery for a wide range of conditions. Smaller incisions mean faster recovery, less pain, and shorter hospital stays.

  • Ovarian cyst removal & management
  • Fibroid removal (Myomectomy)
  • Endometriosis treatment
  • Dysfunctional uterine bleeding
  • Ectopic pregnancy management
  • Pelvic floor disorder surgery
🧬

IVF & Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive fertility workup and advanced IVF treatment in collaboration with Dr. Manan Gupta (MBBS, MS), IVF Specialist & Laparoscopic Surgeon at Elantis Hospital, New Delhi.

  • Male & female infertility evaluation
  • Ovulation induction & monitoring
  • IUI – Intrauterine Insemination
  • IVF / ICSI treatment cycles
  • Embryo transfer & cryopreservation
  • Fertility counselling & emotional support
🌸

PCOD / PCOS & Women's Wellness

Holistic management of polycystic ovarian disease including lifestyle modifications, medical management, and fertility planning for women with PCOD/PCOS.

  • Detailed hormonal profiling & ultrasound
  • Medical & surgical management of PCOD
  • Menstrual irregularity treatment
  • Contraception counselling
  • PAP smear, colposcopy & cervical cancer screening
  • Menopause management & HRT
